Onion Rings

We couldn't get enough of these crunchy onion rings in the EatingWell Test Kitchen. Try any seasoning
blend that you have on hand to add flavor to the breading or substitute 1 teaspoon salt instead. Seasoned
whole-wheat breadcrumbs are available in some supermarkets and natural-foods stores. If you can find
them, try them in place of the plain breadcrumbs and seasoning blend.
